Bio

LE Doan Hoang is currently an Associate Professor with the school of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Aizu (UoA), Japan. He received the B.E. degree in Electronics and Communications from HUST, Vietnam in 2016, and the M.E and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from UoA, Japan, in 2018 and 2021, respectively. From Oct. 2021 to Mar. 2023, he was a Postdoctoral Researcher with Computer Communications Laboratory, UoA.

He was a recipient of a number of awards, including the 2018 KICS/IEEE ICTC Best Paper Award, 2019 IEEE VTS Tokyo Chapter Young Researcher Encouragement Award, 2019 IEEE ComSoc Sendai Chapter Student Excellent Research Award, young researcher to participate in the 9th Heidelberg Laureate Forum 2022, 2022 IEEE ICCE Best Paper Award, and 2023 ICETC Best Poster Award.

Dr. Hoang Le also served as a Chair of the IEEE University of Aizu Student Branch and a committee member of the IEEE Sendai Young Professionals Affinity Group. He is a member of IEEE and IEICE.
